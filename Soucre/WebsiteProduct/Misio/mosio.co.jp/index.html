SHIOGAMA NO MOSHIO

PRODUCT

塩作りを地名の由来にもつ宮城県塩竈市。
『塩竈の藻塩』は、この地に語り継がれる
伝統的な製塩法で炊き上げた、
奥深い味わいの藻塩です。

Shiogama City’s name comes from the Japanese word for salt making.
Our seaweed salt has a deep flavor and is produced using traditional salt-making techniques passed down from long ago.

VIEW PRODUCT

ABOUT

宮城県の小さな港町・塩竈に息づく、
塩作りの歴史と文化、伝統的な製塩法についてご紹介します。

Shiogama is a small port city in the prefecture of Miyagi.
We’d like to introduce you to the connection between Shiogama-produced salt and the people that live there.

ご家庭で気軽にお楽しみください。

With simple recipes, you can enjoy in your own home the flavor of Shiogama Seaweed Salt that we’ve spent time and effort to produce.
